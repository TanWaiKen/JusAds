FYP REPORT WRITING GUIDELINES (SOC & SOT) 
 
COVER PAGE 
Please follow the correct format for the cover page that has been prepared. You may find it in 
the designated folder that has been prepared by your respective FYP managers.  
 
Note:  
• If you are doing a dual program degree, you MUST include the DMU logo and APU 
logo on the cover page.  
• If you are not doing a dual degree program, kindly ensure to include only the APU 
logo on the cover page (put it on the right side). 
• Do not add header and footer to the cover page. 
 
Notes for Project title: 
• The number of words should be no more than 15 words. 
• Please ensure that the students lock down their FYP titles at the IR stage and there 
should not be any changes of titles in their actual FYP itself.  
• Please also make sure the titles chosen have the proper action verbs. While titles 
should be concise and informative, they should also be informative enough to convey 
the main focus of the study. It should avoid using abbreviations. 
• Do not include abbreviation/acronym (must provide full name). 
• Do not include special characters:; -? !, . / 
• Try to limit max 15 words in your FYP Title. 
• Write your FYP title with capitalized each word except conjunction words. 
• The project title should at least contain an action verb – Investigating, Exploring, 
Confirming, Designing, Developing, Defining, Identifying, Establishing – so on and 
so forth. 
• The title should describe the project area, outcomes, solutions, what the student has 
achieved or discovered, and the methodology used. 
• The project title should NOT contain the product name, game name, system name or 
model name. There is no point in doing that. 
• The project title should NOT contain an abbreviation or acronym without its full 
name. 
• It should NOT be something like – A Car Park Management System. It should be 
something like An Artificial Intelligence (AI) Enabled System to Reduce the City 
Centre Parking Problems During Peak Hours. 
• It should NOT be something like – Xuria – A Rogue-Like Game. It should be 
something like Exploring the Effects of Diminishing Gameplay Core Loop 
Systems to Create Engaging Player Experience in Platform Games.

ABSTRACT PAGE 
An abstract section is a one-paragraph summary of a research project. An abstract may 
contain the following information: 
• The purpose of the project identifying the area of study to which it belongs. 
• The research problem that motivates the project. 
• The methods used to address this research problem, document, or evidence 
analysed. 
• The conclusion reached or, if the research is in progress, what the preliminary 
result of the investigation suggests. 
• The significance of the project. 
• What are the mapping SDG components that can be related to the project?  
• Maximum 6 keywords to be included at the bottom of the abstract. 
• Note that the word limit is 200 words.
