# Poster Ad Generation Guide

## Introduction
The Poster Ad Generation tool is designed for static posters, banners, and single-frame visual creatives. It focuses on converting a brand message into a single visual composition with strong copywriting.

## Structured Copywriting Frameworks
1. **AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)**:
   - **Attention**: Catchy hook / headline.
   - **Interest**: Relatable problem or opportunity.
   - **Desire**: Show value proposition/benefits over features.
   - **Action**: Direct CTA ("Order Now", "Learn More").
2. **PAS (Problem, Agitate, Solution)**:
   - **Problem**: Highlight a clear pain point.
   - **Agitate**: Focus on the cost or annoyance of leaving it unfixed.
   - **Solution**: Present the product as the ultimate fix.
3. **FAB (Features, Advantages, Benefits)**:
   - **Features**: Product attributes.
   - **Advantages**: Why these attributes matter.
   - **Benefits**: Life improvement for the customer.

## Creative Prompt Parameters
- **Layout**: Clear typography, high-contrast text placement, focused product display.
- **Modesty Guidelines**: For conservative markets, display the product via packaging/mannequins rather than models.
- **Model**: `imagen-3.0-generate-002` or `imagen-4.0-generate-001`
- **Aspect Ratio**: `1:1` (Square) or `4:5` (Vertical).
