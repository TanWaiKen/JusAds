# Promo Video Ad Guide

## Introduction
The Promo Video Ad tool is used for feature announcement commercials, flash sales, and brand highlight clips. It focuses on clarity, dynamic visuals, and a professional, promotional voice.

## Panel Structure
- **Frame 1**: Major announcement / discount offer (e.g. "MIDYEAR SALE - 50% OFF").
- **Frame 2**: Key feature #1 (visual showcase).
- **Frame 3**: Key feature #2 (value proposition).
- **Frame 4**: Limited time urgency / scarcity trigger (e.g. "Only 50 units remaining!").
- **Frame 5**: Purchase destination details and CTA.

## Assembly
- Stitch the visual frames together.
- Add upbeat promotional backing music or high-energy voiceover voice tracks generated via ElevenLabs.
