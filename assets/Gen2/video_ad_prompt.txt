JUSADS VIDEO GENERATION PROMPT — EVERDRY TRAVEL CONFIDENCE

GOAL
Create a sensitive, dignified 15-second 9:16 video for TikTok, Instagram Reels,
and YouTube Shorts. The campaign is for adults aged 25–55 who face long journeys,
delays, and demanding travel schedules.

REFERENCE ASSETS
- Product.png = PRIMARY PRODUCT REFERENCE. Preserve the EverDry pack designs,
  colours, proportions, product category, and visible branding.
- Diaper.jpeg and Diaper Poster.jpeg = PRODUCT / VISUAL REFERENCES. Use only
  details visibly supported by these assets.
- nimbushealthco_logo.jpg = COMPANY LOGO REFERENCE. Do not replace EverDry
  product branding with the company logo or imply a different product name.

APPROVED MESSAGE
“Travel with confidence. Stay present for every moment.”

TONE
Premium, cinematic, warm, calm, human, and respectful. Never embarrass the
subject, focus on bodily functions, infantilise adults, or use fear and shame.
Do not make medical, leak-proof, absorbency-duration, freshness-duration, or
health claims unless separately verified.

DIRECTOR GRID
Use exactly one complete 2×2 grid with four scenes. Script and generated video
must map one-to-one to the grid. Total duration: 15 seconds.

SCENE 1 — 0:00–0:05 — RELATABLE TRAVEL HOOK
- Airport departure board changes to “DELAYED”.
- An adult traveller pauses, then calmly checks the time.
- Voice: [gently] “Travel days do not always follow the plan.”
- On-screen text: “Plans change.”

SCENE 2 — 0:05–0:10 — QUIET PRODUCT SUPPORT
- Respectful packing moment: the referenced EverDry pack is placed discreetly
  into carry-on luggage.
- Do not show the product being worn or expose the body.
- Voice: [reassuring] “Prepare in a way that helps you feel ready.”
- On-screen text: “Prepare with confidence.”

SCENE 3 — 0:10–0:13 — STAY PRESENT
- Traveller relaxes near the gate and reconnects with a companion.
- Warm natural light; authentic, non-medical environment.
- Voice: [warmly] “Stay present for every moment.”
- Subtitle must match exactly.

SCENE 4 — 0:13–0:15 — PRODUCT + CTA
- Clean hero shot using the exact referenced packaging.
- On-screen text: “EverDry” and “[APPROVED CTA REQUIRED]”.
- Voice: [confident] “EverDry.”
- Hold product and CTA until the last frame.

AUDIO
Original copyright-safe cinematic music: light airport ambience, a soft delay
chime, then a warm resolving musical lift. Dialogue remains clear. Preserve
ElevenLabs V3 emotion tags.

NEGATIVE PROMPT
No medical setting, hospital cues, humiliation, wet clothing, visible accident,
childlike styling, unrealistic mobility, invented protection duration, “100%”
claim, leak-proof claim, cure or treatment claim, false testimonials, altered
packaging, mismatched company/product logos, unreadable text, or incomplete 2×2
grid.

REQUIRED CHECK BEFORE GENERATION
The final CTA, market, language strategy, pack variant, product claims, and
company-logo relationship must be approved. Do not publish with the CTA
placeholder.
