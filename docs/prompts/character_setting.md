Character Turnaround & Reference Sheet Prompt
Base Concept & Layout:
A high-precision, clean, and minimalist character design sheet / character three-view reference page. Pure white background, overall layout well-aligned with proper margins. Think of a professional character design sheet or a character turnaround board. The layout must be neat and clear with distinct information zoning, a realistic and high-end texture, uniform lighting, and strict character consistency.

Left Section (Main Visual Area):
The left side of the canvas features the character's full-body three-view, occupying the primary visual space, displaying respectively:

Front full-body standing pose

Left side full-body standing pose

Back full-body standing pose
Constraint: All three figures must be the exact same character. Facial features, hairstyle, clothing, body type, and height proportions must be completely identical. The standing pose should be natural with arms resting at the sides, suitable for 3D character modeling reference. The camera angle is eye-level, using neutral studio lighting, with no obstructions, no exaggerated perspectives, and no complex backgrounds.

Right Section (Upper Half - Head Angles):
The upper right area contains six character headshots/avatars, neatly arranged, showcasing different head angles of the exact same character, including:

Front-facing headshot

Looking up / Looking down angles

Side profile silhouette

3/4 profile angle / making eye contact
Constraint: Hair must be continuous and clear, with a unified hairstyle, serving as a perfect reference for the character's head design.

Right Section (Lower Half - Details):
The lower right area contains six close-up detail shots, arranged in neat small square grids, highlighting key character details, including:

Close-up of the upper garment's fabric texture

Close-up of the front of the lower garment

Close-up of sleeve/arm tailoring

Close-up of leg or skin texture details

Close-up of the eyes or specific facial features

Close-up of a complete single shoe
Constraint: All detail shots must perfectly match the main character and their clothing. Materials should look realistic with clean details, suitable for character wardrobe modeling reference.

Overall Style Requirements:
Minimalist, professional, realistic, unified, clean, and high-end. It should resemble a professional character design board, a fashion design reference image, or a 3D character modeling reference page. Character edges must be sharp, clothing silhouettes clearly defined, hair strands natural, skin delicate, and materials accurately represented. Ample negative space in the layout, looking like an official asset created by a professional art team.
