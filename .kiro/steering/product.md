# JusAds — Product Overview

JusAds is an AI-powered advertising compliance platform that checks creative assets (video, image, audio, text) against cultural and regulatory guidelines for Southeast Asian markets (primarily Malaysia).

## Core Capabilities

- **Multi-modal compliance checking**: Upload video, image, audio, or text ads and get automated compliance analysis
- **Cultural sensitivity analysis**: Checks ads against market-specific cultural norms (ethnicity, age group, religious considerations)
- **Violation detection & extraction**: Identifies non-compliant segments in video with timestamp-level precision, extracts violation clips
- **Remediation pipeline**: Automated visual and audio remediation suggestions for non-compliant content
- **Risk scoring**: Assigns risk levels and compliance scores (0–100) to each asset
- **Persona-based evaluation**: Uses cultural personas to evaluate ads from the perspective of target demographics

## Target Users

- Advertising agencies creating content for Malaysian and Southeast Asian markets
- Brand safety teams reviewing creative assets before publication
- Marketing teams needing cultural compliance validation

## Key Workflows

1. Upload media → AI compliance check → View violations with clips → Get remediation suggestions
2. Dashboard for managing campaigns, assets, and compliance history
3. Trend analysis across compliance checks
